About CareCompanions

Dedicated to enhancing quality of life through personalized in-home care

Our Mission

At CareCompanions, we believe everyone deserves to age with dignity in the comfort of their own home. Our mission is to provide exceptional, personalized care that allows seniors and individuals with special needs to maintain their independence and quality of life.

Founded in 2010, we've built a reputation for compassionate, reliable care throughout the community. Our caregivers are carefully selected, thoroughly screened, and continuously trained to meet the highest standards of care.

We work closely with families to create customized care plans that address specific needs, preferences, and schedules. From companionship to specialized medical care, we're here to support you and your loved ones.

Our Services

Comprehensive in-home care solutions tailored to your needs

Companionship

Meaningful companionship and emotional support through conversation, activities, and shared interests to combat loneliness and isolation.

Personal Care

Assistance with daily living activities including bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility support to maintain dignity and independence.

Meal Preparation

Nutritious meal planning and preparation according to dietary needs and preferences, including grocery shopping and kitchen cleanup.

Medication Reminders

Timely medication reminders and assistance with medication management to ensure proper dosage and timing as prescribed.

Transportation

Safe transportation to medical appointments, social events, and errands to maintain independence and community engagement.

Light Housekeeping

Assistance with household chores including laundry, vacuuming, dusting, and maintaining a clean and safe living environment.
